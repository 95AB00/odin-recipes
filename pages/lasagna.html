How to make Lasagna

Dish description

Lasagna is one of the most popular and ancient Italian recipes. It consists in very wide, flat sheets of pasta which are stacked together with other ingredients. There are multiple variations of this recipe: the traditional Lasagna Bolognese with meat, Lasagna with pesto and salmon, lasagna with mushrooms, and many others. Here we'll present the recipe for the traditional version.

Ingredients

  • For pasta:
    • Flour - 350 g
    • Egg yolks - 5
    • Eggs - 2
    • Salt
  • For ragu:
    • Tomato sauce - 300 g
    • Minced veal - 300 g
    • Minced pork - 250 g
    • Minced beef - 250 g
    • Carrots - 150 g
    • Onions - 130 g
    • Celery - 130 g
    • Salt & pepper
  • For white sauce:
    • Milk - 1 L
    • Flour - 60 g
    • Butter
    • Salt
    • Parmesan cheese
    • Nutmeg

Steps

  1. Knead the flour with the egg yolks and a pinch of salt, then let it rest for 1 hour
  2. Roll out the dough, cut it in rectangles, and boil them in salted water for 2 minutes
  3. Brown the veal, pork, and beef meats for 15 minutes with a pinch of pepper and oil
  4. Then, add the minced carrots, onions, and celery
  5. After 5 minutes add the tomato, another pinch of salt, and let it slowly cook with low heat for 2 hours
  6. In the meantime, melt the butter in a different pan and mix it with flour and nutmeg
  7. Cook this compound for 6-7 minutes while steering and slowly adding milk
  8. Compose the lasagna by alternating a layer of white sauce, a layer of pasta, and a layer of ragu
  9. On top of the last layer, add a generous portion of grated parmesan cheese
  10. Lastly, cook the lasagna in the oven for 15 minutes at 190Â°C
